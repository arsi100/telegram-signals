Cryptocurrency Trading Signal Application

This application is running on Firebase Cloud Functions and monitoring cryptocurrency markets to generate trading signals.

Signals are delivered via Telegram during the specified market hours.

Features

  • Tracks open positions in Firestore
  • Runs during optimal market hours
